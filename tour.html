Vietnam Beach Vacation 21 days

Deep Insight Laos Vietnam Cambodia Tour 3 weeks

Pick this package for a perfect blend of fun and relaxation on your holiday in Laos, Vietnam, and Cambodia. Spend a wonderful day in Vientiane with a delectable lunch. While in Cambodia, discover ancient temples of the Angkor Complex and pristine beaches in Song Saa Private Island. When traveling to Vietnam, explore the best of Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh in all of its color and glory on this vacation, stroll around for thriving nightlife and endless shopping, explore the mesmerizing tropical beach of Phu Quoc and enjoy an excursion amidst the breathtaking natural scenery.

Tour Plan

Download plan

Day 1: Vientiane Arrival

After arrival at Vientiane Airport, you will meet up with your guide and transit to your hotel. Vientiane holds a different vibe from other capital cities. It appears to be much more tranquil and peaceful with large boulevards and rare traffic jams. Today, you will travel to the famous Buddhist temples, namely Wat Sisaket, Wat Simuang, and Wat Prakeo. The trip also includes stops at the golden-covered That Luang Monument - a Laos’ national landmark and Patuxai (Victory Gate) resembling France’s worldwide famous Arc de Triomphe. The tour ends with a sumptuous dinner at a local restaurant before your free time to unwind and an overnight stay in your hotel in Vientiane.
